# MiracleMeter Feature Ideas

## Core Tracking Features

### Advanced Birth Details
- Apgar score tracking (1min/5min)
- Birth weight/length/head circumference
- Complications tracking with predefined categories
- NICU admission tracking
- Cord blood banking status
- Skin-to-skin contact duration
- First latch timing for breastfeeding

### Location & Facility Management
- GPS location auto-capture (with privacy controls)
- Favorite facilities list with quick-select
- Room number tracking
- Shift tracking (day/night/weekend)
- Travel distance calculator to births

### Team Collaboration During Birth
- Secondary attendant tracking
- Student/trainee participation notes
- Handoff notes between shifts
- Team member role assignments
